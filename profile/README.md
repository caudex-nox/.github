# Caudex

###### Growing roots for future software.

We are **Caudex**, a group of 4 software development students from **Santa Fe, Argentina** 🇦🇷.

Our goal is to put into practice the knowledge we’ve gained throughout our studies by building projects that help us grow both **technically** and **personally**, strengthening teamwork and problem-solving skills along the way.

## 🚀 What we do

At Caudex, we work on projects across different areas of software development:

* 💻 **Low-level programming** (C, C++)
* 🌐 **Fullstack web development**
* 🧩 Using modern **frameworks and libraries**
* 🗄️ **Databases**
* ⚙️ **Operating systems and low-level concepts**
* 🧠 Applying **software engineering principles**

We aim to balance theory and practice, turning what we learn into real-world solutions.

## 🛠️ Our philosophy

###### Learn by building.

We believe the best way to truly understand something is by applying it in real projects and collaborating as a team.

* 📌 We follow **agile methodologies**
* 🤝 We value **teamwork**
* 🔄 We iterate, fail, learn, and improve
* 📈 We focus on continuous growth

## 🌿 Why "Caudex"?

A *caudex* is the base or trunk of a plant, where new growth emerges from.

It reflects our core idea:

> building a strong foundation of knowledge from which we can grow and expand as developers.

## 💡 Goal

Our main focus isn’t just the final result, but the process.
We treat every project as a chance to **learn, experiment, and improve** through real-world practice.
We aim to understand not just how things work, but why, while also developing key soft skills like **communication, teamwork, and adaptability**.

## 🧭 Technical approach

We explore both **low-level** and **high-level** technologies to build a well-rounded understanding of software.
From system-level concepts with languages like **C/C++** to modern web development, we focus on choosing the right tools for each project while continuously expanding our skills.
